REGISTER
Sign in or create an account to start playing.
CHOOSE DIFFICULTY
& CATEGORY
Choose difficulty level of trivia questions and the category of trivia questions.
ANSWER QUESTIONS
Answer the questions to accumulate points and redeem rewards using points. Remember you have a limited time to answer each question.
DISCOVER LOCATIONS
Accumulate at least 1000 points to discover locations. The more points you accumulate the more locations will be available to you.
REDEEM POINTS
Using the accumulated points, redeem points for rewards by scanning bar code at select stores.
